Description
===========
- The OSIRIS-REX Camera Suite (OCAMS) is a set of three cameras designed to image asteroid Bennu from different distances at different resolutions.
    - PolyCam is a long-range camera designed to image the asteroid from a distances between 200â€”2 million km.
    - MapCam provides high-resolution maps of the asteroid's surface
    - SamCam documents the maneuvers and acquisition by the TAGSAM sampling system.
- All cameras use identical detector arrays but have focal lengths separated by a factor of 5.
- The detector is a 1024 x 1024 active pixel CCD array with 6.5 x 8.5 micron pixels.

URL:
https://arcnav.psi.edu/urn:nasa:pds:context:instrument:ocams.orex


OCAM Data Description
=====================

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Camera Suite (OCAMS). OCAMS is a suite of scientific cameras used for the characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu.

URL: https://arcnav.psi.edu/urn:nasa:pds:orex.ocams
